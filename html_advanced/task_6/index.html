Frequently asked questions

What is included in the free plan?

The free plan gives you access to our core library of videos, community forums, and monthly newsletter — no credit card required.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel whenever you like. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period, with no hidden fees.

Do you offer team or studio pricing?

We do. Our Studio and Enterprise plans are built for teams, with shared workspaces, admin controls, and volume discounts available on request.

How do I get support if I have a problem?

Pro and above members have access to priority email support. All members can reach us through the community forum or our help centre.
